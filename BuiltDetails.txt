## What was built

### 🔒 Disclaimer Popup (Disclaimer.jsx)
- Appears on first page load using localStorage — once agreed, it never shows again
- Dark overlay with a centered modal, navy header with gold firm name
- Numbered disclaimer clauses (same pattern as Kochhar & Co.)
- "Agree and Enter" button with hover effect

### 🧭 Navbar (Navbar.jsx)
- Fixed to top, dark navy with gold accent underlines on hover
- Links: About Us · People · Practice Areas · Knowledge Center · Offices
- Hamburger menu appears below 900px — animated into an ✕ when open
- Gains a deeper shadow on scroll via scrollY listener

### 🌟 Hero (Hero.jsx)
- Full-viewport dark navy section with diagonal line texture
- 4-slide auto-rotating text (every 5 seconds) with fade/slide animation
- Dot indicators for manual navigation
- Two CTA buttons + animated scroll arrow

### 🏛️ About (About.jsx)
- Two-column layout: firm description + 4 animated stat cards (25+ years, 500+ cases, etc.)
- Gold border accent on stat cards

### ⚖️ Practice Areas (PracticeAreas.jsx)
- 12 practice area cards in a 4-column responsive grid
- Cards lift and show gold bottom border + "Learn more →" on hover
- Collapses to 3 → 2 → 1 columns on smaller screens

### 🦶 Footer (Footer.jsx)
- 4-column grid: Brand + Quick Links + Practice Areas + Contact
- Social icons (LinkedIn, X, Facebook)
- Disclaimer strip with the Bar Council notice
- Copyright bar with Privacy Policy / Terms of Use / Cookie Policy links

### 🎨 Global Theme (index.css)
- Navy (#0d1b2a) + Gold (#b8963e) color palette via CSS custom properties
- Georgia serif for headings, system-ui for body text
- Utility classes: .container, .section, .section-title
